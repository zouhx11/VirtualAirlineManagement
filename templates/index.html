Understanding the Map

Your command center shows real-time flight operations across the globe. Here's what you'll see:

Major airports worldwide
Departing flights (just took off)
En route flights (cruising)
Arriving flights (approaching destination)

Tip: Click on any aircraft to see detailed flight information and track its progress!

Building Your Fleet

Start your airline empire by acquiring aircraft. You have multiple financing options:

Cash Purchase

Buy aircraft outright for immediate ownership

  • No monthly payments
  • Full ownership benefits
  • Higher upfront cost

Lease Agreement

Lease aircraft with lower initial investment

  • Lower upfront cost
  • Monthly lease payments
  • Flexible fleet management

Strategy: Start with leasing to preserve cash, then buy aircraft as profits grow!

Route Planning & Economics

Profitable routes are the heart of your airline. The route system analyzes:

Revenue Factors

  • Passenger demand between cities
  • Ticket pricing (Economy & Business)
  • Flight frequency per week
  • Aircraft capacity utilization

Operating Costs

  • Fuel consumption & prices
  • Crew salaries & training
  • Airport fees & taxes
  • Aircraft maintenance

Goal: Aim for routes with >15% profit margin for sustainable growth!

Weather Challenges

Real-world weather adds unpredictability to your operations. Monitor these conditions:

Rain

Moderate impact on visibility and landing conditions

Storms

Severe weather may force diversions and delays

Fog

Reduces visibility, may cause approach delays

Strong Winds

Creates turbulence, affects fuel efficiency

Strategy: Weather changes every 2-5 minutes, plan flexible operations!
